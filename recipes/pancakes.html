Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Light, fluffy pancakes perfect for a weekend breakfast or brunch. These golden-brown delights are easy to make and pair perfectly with maple syrup and fresh berries.

Prep time: 10 minutes | Cook time: 15 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  2. In a separate bowl, beat egg, then mix in milk, melted butter, and vanilla
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients; stir until just combined (lumps are okay)
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake
  6. Cook until bubbles form on top and edges are dry, about 2-3 minutes
  7. Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side, another 1-2 minutes
  8. Repeat with remaining batter, adding more oil as needed
  9. Serve warm with butter and maple syrup
