Description

A crisp, refreshing salad with seasonal vegetables and a tangy vinaigrette dressing. This versatile recipe works as a side dish or a light main course, and you can customize it with your favorite vegetables and toppings.

Prep time: 20 minutes | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 6 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, shredded
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds or walnuts
  • For the dressing:
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly
  2. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cucumber, tomatoes, bell pepper, red onion, and carrot
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together all dressing ingredients until emulsified
  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to combine
  5. Top with crumbled feta cheese and nuts
  6. Serve immediately
