Description

This classic Italian lasagna recipe features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. Perfect for family dinners, this hearty dish is sure to become a favorite.

Prep time: 30 minutes | Cook time: 45 minutes | Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside
  3. In a large skillet, brown ground beef with onion and garlic over medium heat
  4. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and basil; simmer for 10 minutes
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  6. In baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce
  7. Layer 4 noodles, 1/3 of cheese mixture, and 1/3 of meat sauce; repeat twice
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes
  10. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until golden and bubbly
  11. Let rest 10 minutes before serving
